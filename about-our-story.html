Our Story

We believe recovery deserves a home.

More than treatment. More than housing. A new way forward.

Every person deserves more than a diagnosis. More than a bed. More than a programme to complete. They deserve the opportunity to rebuild a meaningful life.

At Bayit Miklot, we believe lasting recovery happens when clinical care, safe housing, purposeful living, Jewish values, and genuine community are woven together—not offered as separate pieces for someone to navigate alone.

That belief became the foundation of everything we do.

We are not simply another recovery program. We are building a community where healing continues long after treatment ends.

Why We Exist

Across the mental health and recovery landscape, remarkable clinicians, organizations, and volunteers are changing lives every day.

Yet for many people, the journey remains fragmented.

  • Housing is found in one place.
  • Clinical care in another.
  • Recovery support somewhere else.
  • Community somewhere else again.

Bayit Miklot brings these essential pieces together into one coordinated journey—surrounding every community member with the stability, clinical care, relationships, and encouragement needed for lasting recovery.

“Families are often left trying to connect the dots.
We believe there is a better way.”
More Than a Name

Bayit Miklot means Home of Refuge.

In Jewish tradition, a place of refuge was a place of safety, restoration, and hope—a place where people could begin again.

That vision continues to inspire everything we do.

We strive to create a home where every person is treated with dignity, compassion, accountability, and hope—and where recovery becomes the beginning of a meaningful new chapter.

Because healing is strongest when no one has to walk the journey alone.
