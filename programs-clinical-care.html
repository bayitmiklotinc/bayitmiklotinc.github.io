Clinical Care

Evidence-Based Care.
Compassionately Delivered.

Healing is most effective when clinical excellence is paired with genuine human connection.

At Bayit Miklot, our clinical model combines evidence-based mental health care with the warmth of a supportive community, creating an environment where every individual is treated with dignity, compassion, and respect.

We believe the most effective care addresses the whole person—not simply symptoms or diagnoses.

Personalized Care

No two people arrive with the same experiences.
No two recovery journeys are identical.

Every community member receives a personalized treatment plan developed collaboratively with our clinical team, ensuring care is tailored to their unique needs, goals, strengths, and pace of recovery.

As individuals grow, their care evolves with them.

Because recovery is never one-size-fits-all.

Our Clinical Approach

Our integrated model draws upon a range of evidence-based therapeutic approaches, selected because research supports their effectiveness and adapted to meet each individual's needs.

These include:

Cognitive Behavioural Therapy (CBT) Dialectical Behaviour Therapy (DBT) Acceptance and Commitment Therapy (ACT) Internal Family Systems (IFS) Eye Movement Desensitization and Reprocessing (EMDR) Cognitive Processing Therapy (CPT) Mindfulness-Based Stress Reduction (MBSR) Compassion-Focused Therapy (CFT) Trauma-Informed Care

Every therapeutic approach is delivered within an environment of dignity, safety, and compassionate relationships.

Integrated Clinical Support

Clinical care does not happen in isolation.

Our multidisciplinary team works collaboratively to support every aspect of recovery.

Care may include:

  • Mental health assessment
  • Medication management
  • Individual therapy
  • Group therapy
  • Case management
  • Psychiatric consultation
  • Recovery coaching
  • Family involvement when appropriate
  • Ongoing progress reviews

Each person's care is coordinated so that every professional is working toward shared goals.

Treating the Whole Person

Mental health affects every part of life.
So does healing.

Our approach supports growth across every dimension of wellbeing:

Emotional Health Physical Wellness Mental Health Spiritual Connection Healthy Relationships Employment & Purpose Independent Living Skills Community Integration

Because lasting recovery is about building a meaningful life—not simply managing symptoms.

Working Together

Recovery is strongest when families, clinicians, and community work together.

Whenever appropriate, we actively collaborate with referring professionals, therapists, psychiatrists, hospitals, treatment centres, and family members to ensure continuity of care and thoughtful transitions throughout each stage of recovery.

Healing is never the work of one person alone. It is a shared commitment.

Clinical Excellence.
Jewish Values.

Our clinical care is firmly grounded in evidence-based practice while being delivered within a framework of timeless Jewish values.

We believe science and compassion strengthen one another.

  • Clinical excellence provides the knowledge.
  • Community provides the encouragement.
  • Jewish values provide purpose.

Together, they create an environment where healing can truly flourish.

Beyond Treatment

Clinical care is an essential part of recovery.
It is not the finish line.

Our goal is to help every community member develop the confidence, resilience, relationships, and life skills needed to thrive long after formal treatment has ended.

Because true recovery is measured not only by symptom reduction, but by the ability to live a meaningful, connected, and purposeful life.

“Clinical care helps people recover. Compassion, community, and purpose help them flourish.”
