Join the Bayit
Miklot Community

Recovery is not built by one organization alone. It is built by people who care, show up, and choose to make a difference.

At Bayit Miklot, there are many ways to become part of our mission. Whether you volunteer your time, introduce us to your synagogue, connect us with your business, or become a founding partner, every contribution helps create a stronger community where healing and belonging can flourish.

We believe everyone has something valuable to give.

Become a Volunteer

Another Way to Give

Sometimes the greatest gift isn't financial—it's your presence.

Volunteers bring friendship, encouragement, practical support, and genuine human connection that enriches the lives of our residents every day.

Volunteer opportunities include:

  • Become a workout buddy
  • Cook or sponsor a dinner
  • Host Shabbat or holiday meals
  • Mentor a resident
  • Share your professional skills
  • Help with community activities and special events

Building Stronger Communities Together

Businesses can make a lasting impact through sponsorships, employee volunteer initiatives, in-kind donations, and strategic partnerships.

Whether your organization would like to sponsor a program, donate goods or services, or engage employees in meaningful volunteer opportunities, we welcome the opportunity to work together.

Synagogue Partners

Strengthening Jewish Community Together

Synagogues play a unique role in creating belonging and connection.

We partner with congregations, rabbis, educators, and community leaders through hospitality, volunteer opportunities, educational events, referrals, and community programming.

Together, we can ensure that no one walks their recovery journey alone.

Founding Partners

Help Shape the Future of Bayit Miklot

Founding Partners are visionary leaders who believe in building something that will serve the Jewish community for generations.

Their leadership helps establish the foundation upon which Bayit Miklot will continue to grow, innovate, and expand its impact.

If you are interested in making a transformational investment in this vision, we would be honored to begin that conversation.
