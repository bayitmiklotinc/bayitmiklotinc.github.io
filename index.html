Why Bayit Miklot?

Recovery doesn’t happen through treatment alone.

For too long, people have been passed from one service to another—housing in one place, therapy somewhere else, addiction treatment elsewhere, and community often missing entirely.

Bayit Miklot brings these pieces together into one connected journey.

We believe lasting recovery happens when people are supported medically, emotionally, socially, spiritually, and practically—not in isolation, but as part of a caring community.

This isn’t simply another program.
It is a different way of thinking about recovery.

The Bayit Miklot Difference

Four Pillars. One Connected Journey.

🏠

Housing

Safe, stable, Jewish homes where healing can begin. Residents experience dignity, routine, accountability, and belonging from the moment they arrive.
❤️

Addiction Recovery

Recovery support that recognizes addiction is rarely the whole story. We help people build healthy lives—not simply stop unhealthy behaviors.
🩱

Clinical Mental Health

Personalized care coordinated by experienced professionals, with treatment plans built around each individual rather than a one-size-fits-all protocol.
🤝

Community for Life™

Recovery doesn’t end when someone moves out. Residents become lifelong members of a community that continues to offer friendship, purpose, mentorship, celebrations, support, and opportunities to give back.

This is where Bayit Miklot becomes truly unique.
