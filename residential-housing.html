Residential Housing

More Than a Place to Stay.
A Place to Begin Again.

Healing begins when someone finally feels safe enough to unpack more than their suitcase.

At Bayit Miklot, our residences are more than transitional housing—they are therapeutic homes designed to provide stability, structure, dignity, and belonging during one of life's most vulnerable seasons.

Every aspect of our environment has been thoughtfully created to support recovery, emotional wellbeing, and personal growth. From the moment someone walks through our doors, they are welcomed not simply into a house, but into a caring community where they are valued, supported, and never defined by their past.

A Home Designed for Healing

Recovery requires more than a roof overhead.

  • It requires consistency.
  • Safety.
  • Healthy relationships.
  • Purpose.

A place where people can slow down, regain stability, and begin rebuilding their lives one day at a time.

Our homes provide the foundation upon which lasting recovery can flourish.

Structured, Supportive Living

Every community member benefits from a balanced environment that encourages both personal responsibility and compassionate support.

Our homes offer:

  • Beautifully furnished, comfortable living spaces
  • Safe, professionally managed residences
  • Daily structure and healthy routines
  • Individualized guidance and encouragement
  • Shared meals and meaningful connection
  • Life-skills coaching
  • Employment and vocational support
  • Access to clinical and recovery services
  • Opportunities for recreation, fitness, and personal growth

Everything is intentionally designed to help each person develop the confidence, resilience, and practical skills needed for lasting independence.

Healing Happens Together

One of the greatest challenges after treatment is isolation.

At Bayit Miklot, no one has to rebuild their life alone.

Our community members encourage one another through shared experiences, everyday routines, celebrations, setbacks, and personal victories.

Community is not simply something we offer. It is one of the most powerful parts of recovery.

A Place That Feels Like Home

We believe the environment matters.

  • Warm, welcoming spaces.
  • Comfortable living areas.
  • Shared meals.
  • Shabbos and Yom Tov celebrations.
  • Music.
  • Conversation.
  • Laughter.
  • Quiet moments.

These everyday experiences create the sense of belonging that so many people have been searching for.

Because healing happens not only during therapy sessions, but around the dinner table, while preparing a meal together, over a morning coffee, or simply through knowing someone genuinely cares.

Preparing for Life Beyond Our Walls

Our goal is never dependence. It is confidence.

As each community member grows, we support them in developing the practical skills needed for independent living, including employment readiness, financial responsibility, healthy relationships, emotional wellbeing, and continued recovery.

Success is measured not by how long someone stays with us, but by how confidently they move forward.

A Home for Today.
A Community for Life.

Leaving Bayit Miklot does not mean leaving the community.

Our community members remain connected through alumni gatherings, mentorship, volunteer opportunities, Shabbos meals, Clubhouse events, and lifelong relationships.

  • Recovery continues.
  • Friendships continue.
  • Community continues.

Because everyone deserves a place they can always call home.

“A house provides shelter. A home provides hope. At Bayit Miklot, we believe every person deserves both.”
