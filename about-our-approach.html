Our Approach

A Philosophy of
Integrated Care

We believe people heal best when every part of their life is cared for together.

Photo placeholder
Recovery and Healing with a Human Heart

We combine modern evidence-based therapies with genuine relationships, compassionate support, and personalized care.

  • Care Built Around the Person Not just the condition.
  • Community strengthens recovery.
  • Jewish values provide meaning and purpose.

Together they create an environment where people are treated not as diagnoses, but as whole human beings.

Mental Health Recovery Housing Family Purpose Community

These are not separate challenges. They deserve an integrated response.

At Bayit Miklot, we do not ask people to move from one service to another.

We bring evidence-based clinical care, residential support, recovery, Jewish values, vocational development, and lifelong community around the individual.

Because lasting healing happens when every part of a person's life is cared for together.

Train each person according to their own way.

More Than a Programme

We don't measure success simply by completing treatment.

We measure it by restored relationships.

  • Renewed purpose.
  • Growing confidence.
  • Healthy independence.

And a community that continues long after formal treatment has ended.

Because recovery isn't the destination.
It is the beginning of a life worth building.
